1. Information We Collect About You.

When browsing our website, Atix Labs will collect and store the internet protocol (IP) address of your computer and the internet address of the site from which you directly connected to the Atix Labs site. Atix Labs will also collect and store Cookies. "Cookies" are text files placed on your computer's browser to store certain preferences and information, which can improve your browsing experience on Atix Labs websites. Cookies can be used to personalize your experience on the website, such as recognizing you by name when you return. Atix Labs does not use cookies to track or collect personally identifiable information from website users. If you wish, the instructions for your browser will allow you to make certain settings on your computer to warn you before a cookie is stored, block all cookies, or delete cookies from your hard drive.

You can choose to write to us or complete certain forms or data requests online, which provide your name, phone number, email address, work and related information.
